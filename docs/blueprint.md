# **App Name**: Blossom Bonds

## Core Features:

- Interactive Invitation: Display wedding details (bride & groom, akad & resepsi) in an engaging mobile-first format.
- RSVP Functionality: Allow guests to RSVP directly through the website.
- Location and Directions: Provide the location of the wedding events and integrate with a map service for easy directions.
- Photo Gallery: Share photos of the couple.
- Music Suggestion: Let guests suggest songs that should be played during the party.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft Lavender (#E6E6FA) to evoke a romantic and delicate feel, in alignment with wedding themes.
- Background color: Light Cream (#F5F5DC), providing a warm and gentle backdrop for the colorful floral designs.
- Accent color: Muted Rose (#D8BFD8), for highlighting key interactive elements, complementing the lavender primary color.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a serif font known for its elegant, intellectual, and contemporary feel, fitting for wedding invitations.
- Use delicate floral-themed icons for navigation and interactive elements.
- Mobile-first design with a focus on vertical scrolling and easy tap targets.
- Subtle animations for transitions and loading states to enhance user experience without being intrusive.
